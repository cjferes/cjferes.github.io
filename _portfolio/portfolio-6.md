title: "Acquisition PHY Algorithms for MIMO Wireless Sensor Networks"
excerpt: "Wireless sensor networks (WSN) are critical to enable large-scale IoT applications such as smart cities, environmental monitoring, and others. We proposed the use of multi-antenna technologies to improve the energy efficiency of WSN nodes by exploiting spatial diversity to reduce power consumption. We enable such point-to-point PHY links by acquisition algorithms that use all available antennas to detect incoming packets, perform carrier & timing recovery, and MIMO channel estimation in low SNR conditions.  <br/><img src='/images/image_project1.png'>"

Designed ad-hoc Frame Detection, Carrier Recovery, Timing Recovery, and Channel Estimation algorithms for beamforming MIMO WSN, which exploited spatial diversity to improve energy consumption in low SNR conditions. Developed a fixed-point Simulink testbed, design architectures, and guidelines for FPGA implementation.
